Coffee Is a Chemical System

Coffee is not flavor. It is potential. A roasted bean contains over a thousand chemical compounds.

Brewing does not create flavor. It reveals, dissolves, and balances compounds that already exist.

Acids provide brightness. Sugars provide sweetness. Melanoidins provide body. Phenolics provide bitterness.
