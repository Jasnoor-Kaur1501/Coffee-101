Methods Are Not Recipes

Brew methods are not instructions. They are systems.

Every method is a way of controlling how water meets coffee.

Espresso, pour-over, cold brew — these are not drinks.

The Two Brewing Families

Almost every brew method belongs to one of two families.

Immersion — coffee sits fully in water.

Percolation — water flows through coffee.

Immersion maximizes uniformity. Percolation maximizes control.

Pressure vs Gravity

Some brewers rely on gravity. Others force water through coffee.

Gravity is slow and clear. Pressure is fast and heavy.

The force decides the cup.

Contact Time Spectrum

Extraction can take seconds or hours.

Short emphasizes acids. Long emphasizes sugars and bitters.

Time is chemistry.

Choosing a Method Is Choosing a Philosophy

Do you want clarity or texture?

Methods don’t change taste. They change relationship.
